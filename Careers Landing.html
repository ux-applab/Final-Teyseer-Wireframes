Why Teyseer

For more than fifty years, Teyseer Group has built careers the same way it has built Qatar — patiently, and to last. A career here means joining 18 specialist companies across five sectors, working with the world's leading brands, and growing within one of the nation's most trusted Qatari groups.

From automotive engineering and enterprise IT to construction, manufacturing and facility management, our people are given the training, mobility and responsibility to do their best work — and the stability of a group that has never stopped investing in its talent.
