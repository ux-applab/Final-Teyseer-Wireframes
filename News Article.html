Teyseer Motors Selects SAP for AI-Driven Digital Transformation

Teyseer Motors has partnered with SAP to implement an integrated dealership management platform unifying pre-sales, sales, and aftersales across all brands — with embedded AI capabilities and regional expansion ambitions built in from day one.

Teyseer Group Communications
Thursday, 14 May, 2026 · 4 min read
[Hero Image — Teyseer Motors SAP Partnership Signing]
Teyseer Motors executive team at the SAP partnership announcement, Doha · Thursday, 14 May, 2026

Doha, Qatar — May 2026. Teyseer Motors W.L.L., one of Qatar's leading automotive distributors and a subsidiary of Teyseer Group, has selected SAP to deploy a comprehensive, AI-enabled dealership management platform across its entire brand portfolio.

The implementation will unify all core business functions — from pre-sales enquiries and vehicle configuration to sales contracts, aftersales service, spare parts management, warranty processing, and financial operations — into a single, integrated technology backbone powered by SAP S/4HANA.

"This partnership with SAP is a defining moment for Teyseer Motors. We are building the infrastructure for the next 30 years — one platform, one truth, every brand, every customer."
— Teyseer Motors Leadership, May 2026

A Single Platform Across All Brands

Teyseer Motors currently distributes three internationally acclaimed automotive brands in Qatar — Suzuki, Peugeot, and GWM (including the Haval, Tank, and GWM Poer sub-brands) — with Farizon commercial electric vehicles added to the portfolio in 2025.

Each brand has historically operated on separate systems, creating fragmentation across customer records, inventory, service histories, and financial reporting. The SAP implementation will consolidate all of this into a single source of truth — enabling Teyseer Motors' teams to serve customers consistently, regardless of which brand they drive.

Embedded AI and Regional Ambitions

A key differentiator in the selected solution is the embedded AI capability within the SAP platform. Teyseer Motors will leverage AI-assisted tools for:

  • Predictive aftersales scheduling — anticipating service needs before customers request them
  • AI-driven lead scoring and sales pipeline management across all showrooms
  • Automated spare parts demand forecasting, reducing overstock and out-of-stock incidents
  • Intelligent financial close and real-time P&L visibility across all brands and cost centres

The platform is designed with regional scalability in mind. Teyseer Motors has outlined ambitions to expand its distributor operations into additional GCC markets — a move the SAP architecture is built to support from the outset.

"Qatar's automotive sector is maturing rapidly. Operators who invest in integrated, intelligent platforms today will define the customer experience of tomorrow."
— SAP MENA Regional Director

Implementation Partners: IntelliSoft & Indus Novateur

The deployment will be led by IntelliSoft and Indus Novateur as implementation partners, who will configure and roll out the autoX platform — a SAP-powered dealership management system purpose-built for the automotive distribution sector.

The go-live phasing will prioritise Teyseer Motors' highest-volume operations first, with a planned full rollout across all brands within 18 months of project commencement.

Context: Teyseer Motors' Growth Trajectory

The SAP announcement comes during a period of significant expansion for Teyseer Motors. In 2024, the company formalised an exclusive GWM distributorship for Qatar — completing GWM's full GCC footprint — and opened a flagship showroom on Airport Road that saw over 200 vehicle pre-bookings on launch day. In January 2025, it unveiled the all-new Haval H7 at the Qatar Custom Show, priced at QAR 92,900.

The addition of Farizon commercial EVs in 2025 marked the group's first substantive move into electric mobility — a signal of the strategic direction that the SAP investment is designed to support.

About Teyseer Motors W.L.L.

Teyseer Motors is the exclusive distributor of Suzuki (since 1970), Peugeot (since 2020), GWM (since 2024), and Farizon (since 2025) in Qatar. A subsidiary of Teyseer Group, it operates showrooms and service centres across Doha. For more information, visit teymotor.com.
